October - Domestic Violence Month The first federal effort at countering domestic violence was an important part of President Clinton's crime bill. In addition, the President created the Violence Against Women Office in the Department of Justice and established a toll free number (1-800-799-SAFE) which has provided aid to thousands of battered women.

October - Breast Cancer Awareness Month Under the Clinton Administration, breast cancer research has increased by sixty-five percent. The President established key positions at the Department of Health and Human Services, the National Institutes of Health, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, and the Food and Drug Administration that focus specifically on women's health.

Rallies and other special outreach and voter contact events will be held across the country to mobilize women to make a difference in this election. To participate, contact the Clinton/Gore office in your state.
