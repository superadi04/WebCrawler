Clinton/Gore Supporters Speak Out

“This summer I travelled with the Democratic youth bus tour, on a trip around this nation to go out to young people state by state and find out what was on their minds, talk about their hopes, and their dreams.

In Alabama we set out with a group of young people to build a house with Habitat for Humanity. In Los Angeles we joined with representatives of the California Democratic Party to register new American citizens to vote. And in my home state of Maryland, we talked about the environment.”

— Bobby Zirkin
