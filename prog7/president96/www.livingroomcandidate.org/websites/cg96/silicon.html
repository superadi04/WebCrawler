SILICON VALLEY BUSINESS LEADERS TO ENDORSE CLINTON/GORE

***LIVE AUDIO AVAILABLE***

San Jose, CA -- On Tuesday, August 20 at noon PDT, President Clinton and Vice President Gore will receive an endorsement from prominent executives from high technology companies in the Silicon Valley and throughout California. The executives will gather at Adobe Systems in San Jose, while President Clinton and Vice President Gore will join them via telephone from the White House for their announcement. The call from the White House will be pooled press for still photographers only. However, the Adobe Systems location in San Jose will be open to press coverage. Live listen-in-only audio of the call is available by dialing: 1-800-283-1693. This line will allow reporters unable to attend the event to listen to the announcement.

WHAT: Silicon Valley Executives endorse Clinton/Gore

WHEN: Tuesday, August 20, Noon, PDT

WHERE: Adobe Systems, 345 Park (between Almaden and Guadalupe Parkway)

COVERAGE: OPEN PRESS

NOTE: Interested media must arrive no later than 11:45am PDT and bring proper press and photo identification. No prior notification is necessary. General press parking is available in the garage on Park (near Guadalupe).
