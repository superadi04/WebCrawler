PRESS RELEASE
September 27, 1996

NEW MEXICO AD AGENCY TO FILM PRESIDENT CLINTON

WASHINGTON, D.C. -- An Albuquerque Hispanic advertising agency will go to Texas today, September 27, to film President Clinton during his campaign stops in Ft. Worth and Houston.

A. Gutierrez & Associates, Inc., the agency selected by the Clinton/Gore ï¿½96 General Committee to promote its national Hispanic Outreach campaign, will send a film crew to follow the President during campaign stops in Texas. The goal is to get footage of the President in a Southwestern setting and, in particular, within the Hispanic community, to be used in television and print advertisements to run throughout the country.

Armando Gutierrez, who is president of the agency, said that he was pleased to have been selected to handle Hispanic outreach efforts. "Hispanics have historically been one of the most loyal segments of the Democratic Party. This effort makes clear that neither the Party nor the President is taking us for granted. Our campaign will include television, radio and print advertising."

The footage to be shot by Gutierrez & Associates will be used in television and print ads to begin running nationally in the next few weeks. J. Wiscovitch Associates, Ltd., a New York Puerto Rican agency, will handle creative plans for the East Coast under a subcontract agreement with Gutierrez.
