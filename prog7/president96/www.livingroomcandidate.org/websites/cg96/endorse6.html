PRESS RELEASE
October 14, 1996

VICE PRESIDENT GORE, FIRST LADY HILLARY RODHAM CLINTON KICK OFF FORMATION OF "NATIONAL SENIORS COMMITTEE FOR CLINTON/GORE" ACTIVITIES HELD IN 35 STATES

WASHINGTON, D.C. -- Vice President Gore and First Lady Hillary Rodham Clinton will join thousands of senior citizens from across the country to kick-off the "National Seniors Committee for Clinton/Gore" effort in all 50 states. Vice President Gore will attend an event in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. The First Lady will participate in a roundtable discussion with seniors in Tucson, Arizona.

Events are planned in 35 states to announce the formation of state advisory committees as part of the "National Seniors Committee for Clinton/Gore." There will be advisory committees in all 50 states. With only 21 days until Election Day, the Seniors Committees will focus on increasing senior citizen support for the President and Vice President, identifying voters, and informing communities of President Clinton�s and Vice President Gore�s accomplishments.
