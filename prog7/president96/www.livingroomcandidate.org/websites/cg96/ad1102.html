CLINTON/GORE ‘96 RELEASES NEW ADVERTISEMENT

Today, November 2, 1996, the Clinton/Gore ‘96 campaign announces the release of a new ad entitled, "Guide" The text of the ad follows:

Guide

[chyron - A special message from Reverend Dr. Hugh Tobias]

I’m Reverend Hugh Tobias. I’ve been in the ministry for 22 years.

Don’t be misled by last minute Voter Guides distributed outside our churches.

President Clinton signed the Religious Freedom Restoration Act protecting churches from government interference.

Signed a tough new law to crack down on child pornography and required teen mothers on welfare to stay in school or lose benefits.

How you vote is up to you.

But don’t let the politicians fool you with untrue Voter Guides.
