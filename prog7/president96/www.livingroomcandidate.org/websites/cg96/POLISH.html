POLISH AMERICAN LEADERS FORM

NATIONAL CLINTON/GORE �96 STEERING COMMITTEE

CHICAGO -- The Clinton/Gore campaign today announced the formation of "Polish Americans for Clinton/Gore" at a news conference in Chicago, Illinois. Clinton/Gore Ethnic Outreach Director Ilir Zherka joined Polish American leaders to kick off the committee.

Members of the committee are distinguished Polish American leaders from Connecticut, Illinois, Maryland, Michigan, New Jersey, New York, Ohio, and Pennsylvania. These leaders will develop state steering committees in an effort to increase voter support for reelecting President Clinton and Vice President Gore. With only 28 days left until election day, "Polish Americans for Clinton/Gore �96" will focus on the following activities:

*Informing the Polish American community of the President�s accomplishments;
*Identifying voters in the community; and
*Rallying Polish Americans behind the reelection of the President.

"I am happy that Polish American leaders from across the country have joined together to support my reelection," said President Bill Clinton. "Their support demonstrates our commitment to help Poland build its democracy, to expand NATO, and to strengthen American families and protect our communities. With the help of these leaders, I am hopeful we will carry the day on November 5."
