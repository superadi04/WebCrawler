For Immediate Release: "21st Century Express" Clinton/Gore Schedule






PRESS RELEASE
August 21, 1996

CLINTON/GORE ANNOUNCES PRELIMINARY TRAVEL SCHEDULE FOR PRESIDENT CLINTON’S "21ST CENTURY EXPRESS" TRAIN TOUR

The Clinton/Gore campaign today announced the preliminary travel schedule for President Clinton’s "21st Century Express" train tour. The tour will begin August 25 in Huntington, West Virginia and will end in Michigan City, Indiana on Wednesday, August 28.

As the President travels through America’s heartland, he will highlight the core principles underlying his and Vice President Gore’s leadership agenda: opportunity, responsibility and community. The President will be joined along the route by American heroes whose lives embody these principles.

The schedule is attached. Additional details and media logistics for covering "21st Century Express" events will be released at later dates. For more information, contact the Clinton/Gore press office at 202/496-1037.
