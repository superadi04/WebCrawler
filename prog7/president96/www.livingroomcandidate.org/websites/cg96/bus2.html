FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
September 13, 1996

PRESIDENT CLINTON, VICE PRESIDENT GORE, FIRST LADY HILLARY RODHAM CLINTON AND MRS. TIPPER GORE TO BRING "ON THE ROAD TO THE 21ST CENTURY" BUS TOUR TO SEATTLE, WA AND PORTLAND, OR

President Clinton, Vice President Gore, First Lady Hillary Rodham Clinton and Mrs. Tipper Gore will take their "On the Road to the 21st Century" bus tour to Seattle, Washington. The tour will begin in Seattle on Thursday, September 19 and will conclude in Portland, Oregon on Friday, September 20.

Prior to the bus tour, President Clinton and Vice President Gore will travel to Arizona, stopping at the Grand Canyon, on Wednesday, September 18.

Additional details and media logistics will be made available at later dates.
