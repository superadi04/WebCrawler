PRESS RELEASE
September 4, 1996

PRESIDENT CLINTON TO VISIT
MISSOURI, COLORADO, ARIZONA, AND CALIFORNIA

The Clinton/Gore 96 Campaign announced today President Clinton will travel to Kansas City and St. Louis, Missouri; and Pueblo, Colorado on September 10, 1996. The President will continue on to Phoenix, Arizona and Palo Alto, California on September 11th. On September 12th, the President will travel to Modesto and Los Angeles, California.

Additional details and media logistics on the President's visit to Florida will be made available at later dates.
