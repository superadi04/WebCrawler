MORE THAN 1,100 REPUBLICANS PERSONALLY ENDORSE PRESIDENT CLINTON AND VICE PRESIDENT GORE

Washington, D.C. -- More than 1,100 Republicans have personally endorsed President Clinton and Vice President Gore, announced Clinton/Gore �96 Campaign Manager Peter S. Knight today. The endorsements reach across 25 states and the District of Columbia and include prominent business executives, elected officials, educators and entertainers.

In releasing the list of Republican endorsers, Knight said "We are proud to have such a prestigious group of Republicans showing their support for the President. President Clinton�s record of accomplishment and vision for the future resonates deeply with Americans across the nation," Knight added.

President Clinton and Vice President Gore have been endorsed by Republicans such as actor Kevin Costner; Lanie Wheeler, who is Mayor of Palo Alto, California; Nancy Graham, who is Mayor of Palm Beach, Florida; George Cabot Lodge, who is a professor at Harvard School of Business and son of Henry Cabot Lodge; Thomas Hoving, who is the former Director of the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York; and Chuck Newman, who is President and CEO of TSG-The Source Group. There are large numbers of Republican endorsers in California and New Hampshire with additional names being added.

"It is clear why so many Republicans are enthusiastically supporting the President and Vice President. Before they took office, the deficit had reached a record-breaking $290 billion, the economy was stagnant, jobs were disappearing, and unemployment was high. Under President Clinton�s and Vice President Gore�s strong economic leadership, the deficit has been cut by 63 percent, more than 10.7 million new jobs have been created, annual income is up $1,600, and businesses -- large and small -- are thriving," said Kathryn Haycock a long-time Republican who voted for former President George Bush and is President and CEO of Call-America.

The endorsements were made by Republicans in their individual capacities and are not the endorsements of the businesses for which they work. Attached is the list of Republicans who have endorsed the re-election of President Clinton and Vice President Gore.
