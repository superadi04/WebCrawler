Clinton/Gore '96 Appoints Director of Women's Outreach

Clinton/Gore '96 is proud to announce the appointment of Stephenie Foster to direct women's outreach efforts for the re-election campaign of President Clinton and Vice-President Gore.

Prior to joining the Clinton/Gore '96 team, Foster served as Senator Barbara Mikulski's (D-MD) Chief of Staff. For over two years, Foster worked with Senator Mikulski as her chief advisor, supervising the D.C. and Maryland staff, developing legislative initiatives, and managing the overall operation of Mikulski's offices. Foster is a fromer partner at the law firm of Steefel, Levitt, and Weiss located in San Francisco, CA.

As Director of Women's Outreach, Foster will work with women elected officials, the Democratic National Committee, state parties and state offices, women's groups, and the Office of Women's Initiatives and Outreach of the Clinton Administration. "We are delighted Stephenie has joined us. She brings focus, vision, and understanding of the importance of women participating in electoral politics and how critical they are to the upcoming election," said Ann Lewis, Deputy Campaign Manager.

Foster brings years of political experience to her new job. "I am excited to be here to work on the re-election if President Clinton and Vice-President Gore. This Administration has put women and families at the top of its agenda, and I look forward to highlighting their record with women all across the country," said Foster.

Foster has worked with and been active in many membership organizations including the Women's Legal Defense Fund, the National Conference of Women's Bar Associations, and in 1982 served as a delegate from the District of Columbia to the Democratic Party Midterm Convention.
