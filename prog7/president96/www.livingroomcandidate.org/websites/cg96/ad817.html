For Immediate Release: Campaign Releases New Advertisement




PRESS RELEASE
August 17, 1996

CLINTON/GORE ‘96 RELEASES NEW ADVERTISEMENT


Today, August 17, 1996, the Clinton/Gore ‘96 campaign begins running a new advertisement entitled "Nobody". The text of the ad follows:

Nobody

The Oval Office.

If Dole sits here, and Gingrich runs Congress, what could happen?

Medicare slashed.

Women’s right to choose, gone.

Education, school drug programs cut.

And a risky $550 billion plan balloons the deficit, raises interest rates, hurts the economy.

President Clinton says balance the budget, cut taxes for families, college tuition...

Stands up to Dole and Gingrich.

But if Dole wins, and Gingrich runs Congress, there’ll be nobody there to stop them.

###




Paid for by Clinton/Gore ’96 General Committee, Inc.
