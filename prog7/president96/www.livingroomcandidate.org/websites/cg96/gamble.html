For Immediate Release: Campaign Releases New Advertisement




PRESS RELEASE
August 5, 1996

CLINTON/GORE '96 RELEASES NEW ADVERTISEMENT

Today, August 5, 1996, the Clinton/Gore ‘96 campaign begins running an advertisement entitled "Gamble". The text of the ad follows:

Gamble

America’s economy coming back. Ten million new jobs. We make more autos than Japan. Higher minimum wage.

Now Bob Dole endangers it all with a risky last minute scheme that would balloon the deficit. Higher interest rates. Hurt Families.

President Clinton’s plan: Tax cuts for families. College tuition tax credits. Health insurance you don’t lose changing jobs. Welfare reform. Growth.

President Clinton meeting our challenges. Bob Dole gambling with our future.

###




Paid for by Clinton/Gore ’96 General Committee, Inc.
