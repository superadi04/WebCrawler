President Clinton has an unprecedented record of supporting women and their families. He understands that by improving the lives of American women, we make a vital investment in America's future. His Administration is committed to providing more economic and educational opportunities for women, to providing quality health and child care, and to preventing violence on the streets and at home.

Collecting $11 billion in child support in 1995 — a 40 percent increase from 1992 — by strengthening enforcement of child support laws.
