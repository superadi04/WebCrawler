VICE PRESIDENT GORE, FIRST LADY HILLARY RODHAM CLINTON KICK OFF FORMATION OF "NATIONAL SENIORS COMMITTEE FOR CLINTON/GORE" ACTIVITIES HELD IN 35 STATES

WASHINGTON, D.C. -- Vice President Gore and First Lady Hillary Rodham Clinton today joined thousands of senior citizens across the country to kick-off the "National Seniors Committee for Clinton/Gore" effort in all 50 states. Vice President Gore was featured at an event in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. The First Lady participated in a roundtable discussion with seniors in Tucson, Arizona.

There were activities in 35 states announcing the formation of state advisory committees as part of the "National Seniors Committee for Clinton/Gore." There will be advisory committees in all 50 states.
