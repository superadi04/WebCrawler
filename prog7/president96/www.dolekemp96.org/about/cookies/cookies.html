Pecan Roll Cookies

 

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 lb. margarine, creamed with
  • 4 TBSP powdered sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 TBSP cold water
  • 2 cups broken pecans
Directions:

 

  1. Mix above ingredients together.
  2. Flour hands and roll teaspoons of dough in date - like shapes.
  3. Place on greased cookie sheet and crease with a knife length wise.
  4. Bake at 275 oven for one hour.
  5. Roll on powdered sugar while still warm.
  6. Makes 50 cookies.
